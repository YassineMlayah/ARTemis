Our Vision

Founded in 2023, ARTemis is revolutionizing the digital art market by creating a fair ecosystem where emerging and established talents can thrive.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with artistic expertise to deliver an immersive buying experience while ensuring creators receive fair compensation (85% of sales).

We've already facilitated the sale of over 15,000 original works across 45 countries, creating a dynamic network of 3,200+ verified artists.

ARTemis is more than a marketplace: it's a cultural movement redefining how the world discovers and acquires contemporary art.
